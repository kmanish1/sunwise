What is Solar Energy?

Solar energy is energy from the sun that is converted into thermal or electrical energy. Solar energy is renewable and sustainable, meaning it can be used without depleting natural resources or harming the environment.

Why Use Solar Energy?

There are many benefits to using solar energy. Solar energy is clean and produces no greenhouse gas emissions, making it an environmentally friendly choice. It can also save you money on your energy bills over time, as you can generate your own electricity instead of relying on the grid. In addition, solar panels have a long lifespan and require little maintenance.
