Our aim

Our website aims at empowering people to make more environmentally friendly choices by providing them with the necessary information and tools to switch to solar energy, potentially reducing their carbon footprint, and promoting sustainable living.

Overview

Our website is providing people with the necessary tools and information to help them make informed decisions about converting to solar energy. By offering a solar conversion cost calculator, solar panel cost calculator with subsidy, and EMI calculator, users can quickly estimate the cost of switching to solar and the potential savings they can make. Additionally, our website mentions different financing options, providing users with valuable information to help them fund their solar energy investments.

Easy Input

The website only requires users to input their monthly electricity bill and state, making it a hassle-free experience.

Saves time and effort

The website eliminates the need for users to research multiple sources for information about the cost of converting to solar.

Targeted towards residential areas

The website is designed specifically for residential areas, making it unique from other solar websites that cater to businesses.

Finance options available

The website mentions different financing options available to users, helping them make informed decisions about their investment in solar.
